Professional Paint Protection

Born out of real-world frustration, Paint Guard was developed after tackling a ceiling with 36 downlights. No more stress, just flawless results.

Innovative Design Meets Practical Solution

Paint Guard sits flush against recessed Downlight, Spotlights, LED's, allowing decorators to leave the fixtures in place while painting. It can be washed and reused multiple times, reducing environmental waste.
